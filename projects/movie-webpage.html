Movie Webpage Project

Context / Content

In our SEP class, we had to collaborate and communicate with our partner to make a movie webpage (kind of like a trailer). We made somewhat like a horror movie about an old sweet granny has something in depth of her well.

Reflection

Since our movie webpage can be anything we want, one of the most challenging part of creating this webpage was deciding on what our topic will be about. We were both confused about what our movie should be about, so we took 2 days completing our plan. Through this movie webpage project, my biggest takeaway is the key for communication. During this project, I had to communicate with my partner and discuss about what our topic should be. We had disagreements and agreements, however, we were able to finish it before time and were both satisfied with what we have. Our next steps is to add some more background information about our movie because I feel like there isn't enough information for others to understand or have the interest to continue reading.

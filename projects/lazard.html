Lazard New Visions Academy

Context/Content

The goal of the Lazard New Visions Academy is to provide students with opportunities to develop college and career readiness skills, investigate careers, especially those in the financial services sector, while developing their social capital and learning some fundamentals of financial literacy.

Week 1-2:

Career Readiness Skills & Self Awareness: What does it mean to invest in myself and in others?

Week 3-5:

Financial Literacy and Industry Capstone: What does it mean to financially invest? What does it mean for companies to invest in each other?

Skills Gained

Maturity & Motivation: Completion of program application and interview, motivation to learn, demonstrations of resilience and dedicaion, and have a postsecondary vision.
